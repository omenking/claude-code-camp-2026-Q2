# World Memory

## Midgaard City

### Main Street (E-W spine of the city)
- **West Gate** → west exit (closed gate)
- **Magic Shop** — north of western Main St
- **Mages' Guild entrance** — south of western Main St (guarded — warrior class blocked)
- **Armory** — south of central Main St (armor for sale)
- **Bakery** — north of central Main St (food?)
- **General Store** — north of eastern Main St
- **Pet Shop** — south of eastern Main St
- **Weapon Shop** — north of far-east Main St
- **Guild of Swordsmen entrance** — south of far-east Main St

### Squares
- **Market Square** — center of city (n/e/s/w roads)
- **Temple Square** — north of Market Square
  - Clerics' Guild entrance: west (entry blocked — wrong class)
  - Grunting Boar Inn: east
- **Common Square** — south of Market Square
  - Poor Alley: west
  - Dark Alley: east
  - Dump: south → sewer (pitch black, dangerous)

### Guild of Swordsmen (Warrior's Guild)
- **Entrance Hall** — south of far-east Main St (knight guard)
- **Bar of Swordsmen** — east of entrance (bulletin board, waiter)
- **Tournament and Practice Yard** — south of bar
  - Guildmaster here (sharpening axe)
  - Well leads down into darkness
  - **This is where you practice skills**

### Temple of Midgaard
- Up from Temple Square → Temple of Midgaard (main hall)
  - Reading Room: west
  - Donation Room: east (ATM inside)
  - Altar: north end
  - Back of Temple: north → countryside path

### Other
- **Dark Alley** — east of Common Square
  - Thieves' Guild entrance: south (blocked — wrong class)
  - Levee: east-south → river (need a boat to cross)
- **Poor Alley** — west of Common Square
  - Wall Road: west (city wall, guards)

## Outside Midgaard

### The Great Field
- North of city (via temple back exit)
- **Newbie Zone** entrance — east of the field (2 rooms north of city)
  - Corridor with creepy crawlers and newbie monsters
  - Exits loop back west to an open field
- **Chessboard of Midgaard** — west path (above recommended level, skip for now)

## Navigation Quick Reference
To reach the **Warriors Guild Practice Yard** from Market Square:
1. `east` → Main St (weapon shop area)
2. `south` → Guild of Swordsmen entrance
3. `east` → Bar of Swordsmen
4. `south` → Tournament and Practice Yard (guildmaster here)

To reach the **Newbie Zone** from Market Square:
1. `north` → Temple Square
2. `north` → Temple
3. `north` → Altar
4. `north` → Behind Altar
5. `north` → Great Field
6. `north` → Great Field (with newbie zone sign)
7. `east` → Newbie Zone entrance
8. `north` → Enter corridor
