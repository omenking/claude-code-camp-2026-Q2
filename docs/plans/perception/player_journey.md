## First Time Journey (Andrew Person)

- Spawn in starting location.
- Walk around the safe zone and determine the key points of interest
  - bakery (food)
  - tavern (information)
  - inn (healing, buffs or saving)
  - temple (healing, buffs)
  - equipment shoop (weapons, swords)
  - guilds (training, free gear?)
  - do I spot any free items/gear
  - do I spot any important npcs eg. Mayor
- I would peak into stores and see what things cost
  - when do I have enough money to come back
- Go to guild and train.
- I would ask around town to determine the starting zone to level
- I would check all exits of the town and see what mobs are around to determine starting zones

Pathing
  - How do I make a map
  - How do I refer to that map
  - Where have I been and where I am
  - What key information should I be storing in my map
    - Where are these NPC, or Items, or Stores
      - Putting a pin on the map
      - Do they move? 

Preperation

    - how much food
